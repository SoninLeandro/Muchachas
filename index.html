This is our Story

How it started

Back in 2021 a group of women got together with the objetive of playing and having fun, until the team was formed and decided to go one step forward looking for a new challenge.

Who we are

We are a Latin American team, and we like to keep our traditions to make people feel a bit closer to their homes. Football is what keeps us together while we enjoy our lives in our new home, London.

We are family

While it is a football team, Muchachas is also a family, with lots of people involved working hard to get this group together, having fun and supporting one another.

Next steps

Muchachas FC is present and future! Our Muchachitas team will be built soon so players of any age can join us.

This 5-a-side league, in Hackney Wick, is one of the biggest women's leagues in London. With 6 different categories and almost 50 teams in total, this is a great challenge for any football team. Follow them on Instagram!

A fantastic World-cup-like tournament, with 10 teams playing 9-a-side games and each one representing their own country! Follow the link to find out more about it!

Training Sessions

Who can join?

Anyone is welcome! Whether you're a regular player looking to enhance your skills or a beginner eager to start playing football, this is the ideal place for you!

Days

We have our training sessions every Tuesday, starting at 7:00PM and finishing at 8:30PM. We meet up at 6:45PM to change and warm up. If, for any reason, we're unable to train on Tuesday, we reschedule for Thursday at the same time.

Place

During the summer, our training sessions take place at Hyde Park. However, starting from October 1st, we move our sessions to West Way fields.

Costs

For each training session, we're collecting a £5 contribution from everyone. This helps the team grow and ensures we have all the necessary tools and equipment.
