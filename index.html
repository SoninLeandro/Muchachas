Who we are

We are a Latin American team, and we like to keep our traditions to make people feel a bit closer to their homes. Football is what keeps us together while we enjoy our lives in London

How it started

Back in 2021 a group of women got together with the objetive of playing and having fun, until the team was formed and decided to go one step forward looking for a challenge.

We are family

While it is a football team, Muchachas is also a family, with a lot of people involved working hard to get this group together, having fun and supporting one another.

Next steps

Muchachas FC looking for a bigger group, with more players and specting this team to win titles sooner than later.

Competitions

All Super 5 League World Cup Charity Cup

SUPER 5 LEAGUE

This 5-a-side league, in Hackney Wick, is one of the biggest women's leagues in London. With 6 different categories and over 50 teams in total, this is a great challenge for any football team.

The Inner City London World Cup

A fantastic World-cup-like tournament, with 10 teams playing 9-a-side games and each one representing their own country!

Charity Cup

Not everything is about winning or loosing. That is why Muchachas decided to take part in this Charity Cup to support schools in Argentina, being able to collect donations and send all the possible help to those who needs it the most.

SUPER 5 LEAGUE

Muchachas FC started this journey on May 2023, being this our first competitive tournament. We started with a leveler game, which we won 2 - 0, and it gave us the chance to start playing in the Intermediate league. Follow the link to see the standings as of today!

The Inner City London World Cup

Our team is proud of being the Argentinian representative in the annual London World Cup tournament!

Rivals, not enemies

All the argentinian teams (and everywhere else) together for a great cause. They are all winners here! See how you can help as well clicking in the link.

Training

Days

We train every Tuesday from 19.00 to 20.30. We gather at 18.45 to get changed and warm up. If for any reason we can't train, we reschedule for Thursday same time.

Place

During summer time we are holding training sessions at Hyde Park. From October 1st on, we relocate to West Way fields.

Who can join?

Anyone! Either you play regularly and want to improve your skills, or you just want to get started in football, this is the perfect place for you!

Costs

We are taking a £5 contribution each training session, to help the team grow and making it possible to have all the necessary tools.
